The Shared ledger in BlockChain
Before moving into the specifics, letâ€™s understand the concept of a shared ledger. A ledger is a document wherein all the financial transactions are recorded. A bank maintains a ledger that contains the transaction information whenever you buy something using your credit/debit card. In a shared ledger, transactions are recorded only once across all the business network. Each participant has the duplicate copy of the ledger and they are permissioned to see only the transactions they are authorized to view.
